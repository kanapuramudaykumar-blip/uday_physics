About Me

Professional Journey

Dr. Udaykumar Khanapuram is an accomplished Assistant Professor in the Department of Physics at NIT Warangal.

Research Expertise

My major research work focuses on energy harvesting materials and device fabrication. Today's world is experiencing growing energy and environmental challenges that require developing sustainable and renewable resources. Therefore, new materials that are capable of converting and saving energy are highly desirable. During my Ph.D. work, I worked on the fabrication of electrochromic devices on flexible substrates. We have developed electrochromic devices on flexible substrates with neon-sputtered tungsten oxide films. Currently, we are working on 2D materials, Metal oxides, and Metal-Organic-Framework for different energy harvesting applications.
